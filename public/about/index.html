👉 This is ryluiiwan#

Formerly worked in TikTok influencer marketing (a shameless product peddler, ha), now doing affiliate marketing(2026/3) overseas influencer marketing along with social media operations. I’d like to pick up some Digital Marketing skills (SEO / Google Ads). My undergraduate major was Data Science, so I know a bit of programming such as Python and have some basic data-analysis intuition. After graduating I drifted around for a while and stumbled into the marketing world by accident. In my off hours I like eating, sleeping, wandering through parks, counting stars and gazing at the moon, reading, and having freewheeling conversations with friends. I have a strong urge to understand the underlying nature and patterns of things.

I hope to become a person with real inner strength.

🥂 Wishing Well#

Personal life — I hope to have both a robust body and a peaceful mind, and to live a long life (and I hope this isn’t just a dream).

Career — to do work that is genuinely useful (to me and to others). I believe mutual fulfillment and win-win cooperation is the best principle in human relationships, and I want to keep digging deeper in a particular field over the long haul.
